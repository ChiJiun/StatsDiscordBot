### Problem Statement
As a realtor you want to determine if house style and house location are related. At the 0.05 level of significance, is there evidence of a relationship?
	House Location
House Style	Urban	Rural	Total
Split-Level	63	49	112
Ranch	15	33	48
Total	78	82	160

### Model Solution
1. State: Hypotheses and Parameters
Goal: We want to determine if there is a significant relationship (dependence) between house style and house location at the α=0.05 significance level.
•	Hypotheses:
o	H0: House style and house location are independent (There is no relationship between them).
o	Ha: House style and house location are dependent (There is a significant relationship between them).
•	Significance Level: α=0.05.
________________________________________
2. Plan: Procedure and Conditions
Procedure: Chi-square Test for Independence.
To perform this test validly, we must verify:
1.	Random: We assume the 160 houses represent a random sample from the target population.
2.	Independence: The total sample size n=160 is likely less than 10% of all houses in the region.
3.	Large Counts: All expected counts must be ≥5.
o	Expected Count (E) = Grand TotalRow Total×Column Total
o	Min Expected: (48×78)/160=23.4.
o	Since 23.4≥5, the condition is satisfied.
________________________________________
3. Do: Calculations
Data Summary:
•	Observed (O): Split/Urban=63, Split/Rural=49, Ranch/Urban=15, Ranch/Rural=33.
•	Expected (E): Split/Urban=54.6, Split/Rural=57.4, Ranch/Urban=23.4, Ranch/Rural=24.6.
Calculation:
1.	Test Statistic (χ2):
χ2=∑Ei(Oi−Ei)2
χ2=54.6(63−54.6)2+57.4(49−57.4)2+23.4(15−23.4)2+24.6(33−24.6)2
χ2=1.291+1.229+3.015+2.868=8.403
2.	Degrees of Freedom (df): df=(rows−1)(cols−1)=(2−1)(2−1)=1.
3.	P-value / Critical Value: Using the Chi-square table for df=1,α=0.05:
o	Critical value χ0.052=3.841.
________________________________________
4. Conclude: Interpretation
Statistical Decision: Since the calculated test statistic χ2=8.403 is greater than the critical value 3.841, it falls into the rejection region. We Reject the null hypothesis (H0) at α=0.05.
Contextual Conclusion: There is sufficient evidence at the 0.05 level of significance to conclude that there is a significant relationship between house style and house location.
